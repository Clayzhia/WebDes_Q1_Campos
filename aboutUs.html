HISTORY OF LAILEY&CO.

Lailey&Co. was established in 2024 with a singular focus: to revolutionize the world of lip products.
With a deep-rooted belief in the power of lips to express individuality and confidence, we set out to
create a line of lip products that celebrated diversity and embraced uniqueness.

From the outset, we are driven by a commitment to inclusivity and empowerment. They believed that
every lip deserved to be adorned with products that not only enhanced natural beauty but also
represented the rich tapestry of skin tones and styles.

As the brand gained momentum, its dedication to quality and diversity set it apart in the
industry. we quickly became synonymous with high-quality, inclusive lip products, attracting a
devoted following of beauty enthusiasts who shared their values.

Today, we remain unwavering in its commitment to redefining beauty standards through its lip
products. With a focus on innovation, inclusivity, and self-expression, we continue to empower
individuals to embrace their unique beauty, one lip product at a time.
